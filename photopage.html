P. 000

这里是一些莫名其妙的词句和照片,

可以理解为停不下来的思绪, 也可以什么都不是......

P. 001

立方体, 一个简单的数学模型,
Cube, a simple mathematical model.
我们都是其一部分,
是薛定谔盒子中的某个身影.
We are all part of it,
as some figure in Schrödinger's box.
旋转, 徘徊, 与惴惴不安,
心脏的跳动在宇宙中没有回音.
Spinning, wandering, and apprehensive,
the beating of the hearts echo in the universe.
疯子刻下的一组组烙印清晰可见,
被灵魂的颜色照耀得星星点点.
The engraving carved by the madman is clearly visible,
illuminated by the color of the soul.
声音以光束的形式从门缝中穿透进来,
屋子里神秘的火焰被瞬间熄灭.
The sound penetrated through the door in the form of light,
and the mysterious flame in the room instantly died out.
兴奋与恐惧没有明确的分界线,
混凝土完全有理由占据天空的视野.
There is no clear difference between excitement and fear.
The concrete has every reason to occupy the view of the air.
若是人类的进化再完善一些,
我们之间是否就没有差别.
If human beings evolved a little more,
would us all be the same?

P. 002

黑夜的寂静将你凸显,
在无人知晓的明显焦点.
In the obvious focus of no one knowing,
the silence of the night will highlight you clearly.
遵循着成为人类的客观条件,
Following the objective conditions of being a human,
在凌晨三点望着窗外无言.
looking out the window at three o'clock in the morning.
路标作为回望过去的钥匙,
Road signs serve as keys to looking back at the past.
雨滴牵起两头的红线.
The raindrops connected the red lines at both sides.
轨道是通往异世界的桥梁,
The track is a bridge to another world.
扩散的意识跳入列车与熟悉的一切挥别.
Diffusing consciousness jumped into the train and left everything behind.

P. 003

水雾冲破身体的外壳,
最初的一切如风般穿过胸膛.
The water mist breaks through the shell of the body,
and the first version of everything passed through the chest like the wind.
沿着基因书写的扭转路线,
被灌溉的苔藓轻轻地踏过池塘.
Following a twisted route written by genes,
the irrigated moss gently swims across the pond.
丝线在草丛间默默织网,
Silk threads silently weave nets among the grass,
构建出人眼中的动态视线.
constructing a dynamic field of view for the eyes.
拱起脖子穿过大街,
脑海里还是过往的那一朵莲.
Arch our necks and cross the street,
thinking of the lotus of the past.
阶梯上的铁链在雨中无声击打,
肆意抒发对对泥土的依恋.
The iron chains on the stairs beat silently in the rain,
expressing their attachment to the dirt.
云彩拉起天空的幕布,
便是为构建这单色的多彩空间.
The clouds pull up the curtain of the sky
to build this monochromatic colorful sight.
